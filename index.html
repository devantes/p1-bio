Passion

Defining a single passion can be troublesome, especially when I draw my passion from the world around me. Every experience takes me further, every achievement takes me further, every failure takes me even further. It is in those moments of a successful design sparking the smile of a client, and those moments of attempt after attempt to get a design right that drive me to the brink of insanity. It is in those moments I find my passion.
